Let's start from the simplest rule, probably :) Matching a digit.

There are many cases in daily routines that require us working with numbers that are for some reason trapped within a sequence of characters. Regex will help you find those numbers.

In order to match a single digit you have to specify special character \d or use [0-9] as regular expression pattern. It will match only first digit it finds, to find following digits you should put asterisk (*) symbol right after your chosen regexp pattern for digits. Essentially it means look forward character by character until you reach non-digit or end of given string.
