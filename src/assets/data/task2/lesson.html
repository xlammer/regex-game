Quantifiers in regular expressions define amount of matches of previous rule. For example expression a{2} for string 'aabba' will match only first double a's - this should be easy, right? Let's look at following table:
# Quantifier What it matches
1 {2} Exactly 2 symbols (may be any number)
2 {2,7} Between 2 and 7
3 {2,} 2 or more
