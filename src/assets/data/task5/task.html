For the final task you'll be creating validator for an e-mail address.

Rules are pretty simple:

  • Name part (before @) should consist only of english letters a-z, min length is 2 max length is 6
  • Domain part (after @ and before dot) should consist only of english letters a-z, min length is 3 max length is 8
  • Domain zone (the end part after dot) is either ee or eu

Only 2 out of 5 given e-mails are valid.
