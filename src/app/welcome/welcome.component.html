Welcome to Playtech RegEx game that will teach you some cool stuff today, hopefully ;)

Regular expression (aka RegEx or RegExp) is quite powerful technique used in different kinds of problem solving that require either searching (and/or replacing) sequences of characters in a given string. Have you seen validation of e-mail or phone number fields in the web? That's most probably done by the regular expressions.

It's all about learning regex and writing some patterns today. Until you decided to solve a problem with regular expressions - you had only one problem. Guess what? Now you have two :)
